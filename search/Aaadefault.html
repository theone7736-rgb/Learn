The creation of the universe

Almost everyone thinks that our universe was made from the Big Bang theory, but is that true? This article explores multiple theories.

The Big Bang Theory

The Big Bang theory explains how the universe began from a hot, dense state and has been expanding ever since.

The Expanding Universe

Space itself is stretching, causing galaxies to move farther apart.

The Simulation Theory

This theory suggests reality could be a highly advanced simulation.

The Unknown Theory

Humanity may not yet understand the full structure of reality.
