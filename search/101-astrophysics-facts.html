  6. The moon is not made of cheese, but rock.
  7. The moon was created when a mars sized planet called Thea crashed into proto-earth.
  8. Both the earth and the moon were made about 4.5 million years ago.
  9. The moon can fit in Australia.
  10. The moon is Earth's only natural satellite.
  11. 71% of earth is covered in water.
  12. Humans first walked on the moon in 1969.
